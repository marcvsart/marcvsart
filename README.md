---

A name without a surname, drifting through the informational mass.

Started writing before making music — typewriter at age 7, electronic production at 13.
Two decades of sediment: from Fruity Loops to Ableton, from industrial to glitch-pop,
from journalism to poetry. The work arrives from the tension between **machine and literature** —
spectral pads over spoken word, beats that carry the weight of verse.
Portuguese not as ornament, but as poetic structure.

Audiovisual is the extension. Sound is the origin.
